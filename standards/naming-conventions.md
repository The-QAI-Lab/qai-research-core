# Naming Conventions

All naming must be consistent and operationally descriptive. This ensures clarity and interoperability across the research skill pipeline.

## Folders and Files
- Use lowercase, hyphen-separated names (e.g., `literature-review`)
- No spaces or underscores
- Skill folders: `skills/<skill-name>/`
- Shared assets: `shared/<asset-name>.md`
- Standards: `standards/<standard-name>.md`

## Skill Names
- Must be descriptive, unambiguous, and reflect the operational role
- Avoid abbreviations unless universally recognized in the field
- Align with the skill pipeline and dependencies

## Examples
- `dataset-schema-designer`
- `experiment-design`
- `reviewer-mode`
- `gap-finder`
