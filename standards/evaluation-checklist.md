# Evaluation Checklist

Use this checklist for peer review of all skills and outputs. All criteria must be met for acceptance. Skills must demonstrate reasoning, tradeoff logic, and operational handoff—not just formatting.

- [ ] Follows `standards/skill-spec-template.md` in full
- [ ] Inputs and outputs are explicit, structured, and standards-compliant
- [ ] Output contract is defined, referenced, and followed
- [ ] Prompts are agent-agnostic, operational, and require reasoning
- [ ] References all relevant shared standards and assets
- [ ] Behavioral tests in `tests.md` are operational, realistic, and validate reasoning quality
- [ ] Handles all documented failure modes, uncertainty, and assumption surfacing
- [ ] Surfaces tradeoffs, alternatives, and decision logic where appropriate
- [ ] Handoff artifacts are present, structured, and machine-readable
- [ ] Domain adaptation notes are present and actionable
- [ ] Documentation is complete, precise, and operational
- [ ] Skill is interoperable with upstream and downstream skills
