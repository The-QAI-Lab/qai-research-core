# Paper Structures

Standard research paper outline (IMRaD):
1. Title
2. Abstract
3. Introduction
4. Methods
5. Results
6. Discussion
7. Conclusion
8. References

## Section Guidance
- **Introduction:** Context, rationale, objectives
- **Methods:** Study design, participants, data collection, analysis
- **Results:** Findings, tables, figures
- **Discussion:** Interpretation, limitations, implications
- **References:** All cited works, formatted per `shared/citation-rules.md`

Adapt structure as needed for specific domains or journals, but maintain explicit sectioning and traceability.
