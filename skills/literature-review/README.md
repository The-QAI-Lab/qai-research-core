# Literature Review Skill

A reasoning module for systematic, standards-driven synthesis of academic literature. Goes beyond summary to cluster evidence, map consensus/disagreement, score evidence strength, and surface recurring limitations and open questions for downstream research.

- **Purpose:** Produce structured, auditable, and reasoning-rich literature syntheses for research planning, background, or gap analysis.
- **When to Use:** At project initiation, for manuscript background, or to identify field trends, consensus, and research gaps.
- **When Not to Use:** For non-academic sources, opinion pieces, or when a simple keyword search suffices.
- **Related Standards/Assets:** `shared/citation-rules.md`, `shared/paper-structures.md`, `standards/output-contracts.md`
- **Upstream:** Entry point
- **Downstream:** Gap Finder, Experiment Design
- **Handoff Artifact:** Gap Handoff Block (structured for downstream skills)
- **Reasoning Features:** Evidence clustering, consensus mapping, uncertainty reporting, tradeoff analysis, explicit assumptions
