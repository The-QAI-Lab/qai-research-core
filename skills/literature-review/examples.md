# Literature Review Skill — Examples

## Example 1: Generic Academic
**Input:** Summarize recent literature on deep learning for image segmentation (2019–2023, peer-reviewed). Cluster by methodology, score evidence strength, and produce a Gap Handoff Block.
**Output:**
- Executive Summary
- Evidence Clusters: "Supervised Methods", "Unsupervised Methods", "Hybrid Approaches"
- Consensus/Disagreement Map: e.g., "Consensus on U-Net for medical images; disagreement on GAN-based reliability"
- Evidence Strength Table: methodology, number of studies, strength score
- Limitations and Uncertainties: e.g., "Limited validation on rare diseases"
- Gap Handoff Block: structured list of open questions and limitations for downstream use
- Citations (APA 7th)

## Example 2: Biomechanics
**Input:** Summarize literature on wearable sensors for gait analysis in older adults (2015–2024, English, peer-reviewed). Identify consensus, recurring limitations, and produce a Gap Handoff Block.
**Output:**
- Executive Summary
- Evidence Clusters: "Sensor Types", "Validation Studies", "Population Coverage"
- Consensus/Disagreement Map: e.g., "Consensus on IMU accuracy; disagreement on long-term reliability"
- Evidence Strength Table
- Limitations and Uncertainties: e.g., "Sparse data for non-Western populations"
- Gap Handoff Block: e.g., "Lack of validation in diverse populations"
- Citations (APA)

## Example 3: QAI-Style Applied
**Input:** Produce a structured review of machine learning for injury risk prediction in elite athletes (2018–2023, PubMed/IEEE only). Cluster by approach, map consensus, and produce a Gap Handoff Block.
**Output:**
- Executive Summary
- Evidence Clusters: "Feature Engineering Approaches", "Model Types"
- Consensus/Disagreement Map: e.g., "Consensus on importance of time-series features; disagreement on model interpretability"
- Evidence Strength Table
- Limitations and Uncertainties: e.g., "Small sample sizes, lack of external validation"
- Gap Handoff Block: e.g., "Need for multi-center validation studies"
- Citations (APA)
