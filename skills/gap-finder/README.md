# Gap Finder Skill

A reasoning module for surfacing, ranking, and structuring actionable research gaps from structured literature review outputs. Goes beyond extraction to prioritize gaps by novelty, feasibility, impact, and data accessibility, and produces machine-readable handoff artifacts for downstream experiment design and paper outlining.

- **Purpose:** Surface, rank, and structure underexplored, high-impact research questions for downstream study design.
- **When to Use:** Immediately after a literature review with explicit limitations, uncertainties, and Gap Handoff Block.
- **When Not to Use:** If literature review is missing, unstructured, or lacks explicit limitations/gaps.
- **Related Standards/Assets:** `skills/literature-review/skill.md`, `standards/output-contracts.md`
- **Upstream:** Literature Review
- **Downstream:** Experiment Design, Protocol Writer
- **Handoff Artifact:** Experiment Framing Block (structured for downstream skills)
- **Reasoning Features:** Gap prioritization matrix, candidate research questions, alternative prioritization, explicit assumptions and tradeoffs
