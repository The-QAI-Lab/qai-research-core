# Gap Finder Skill — Examples

## Example 1: Generic Academic
**Input:** Identify and rank research gaps in explainable AI for healthcare (2020–2024, peer-reviewed literature review output attached). Include a prioritization matrix and Experiment Framing Block.
**Output:**
- Gap List: actionable gaps, each with rationale, supporting citations, and traceability to literature review
- Gap Prioritization Matrix: criteria (novelty, feasibility, impact, data accessibility)
- Candidate Research Questions
- Experiment Framing Block: structured summary for downstream skills

## Example 2: Biomechanics
**Input:** Identify and prioritize research gaps in ACL injury prevention (literature review summary attached). Include candidate research questions and Experiment Framing Block.
**Output:**
- Gaps organized by intervention type and population, each with rationale and citations
- Gap Prioritization Matrix
- Candidate Research Questions
- Experiment Framing Block

## Example 3: QAI-Style Applied
**Input:** Extract and prioritize gaps from a review of real-time motion capture for rehabilitation (2022–2024, PubMed/IEEE only). Include alternative prioritization and Experiment Framing Block.
**Output:**
- Actionable gaps mapped to technology readiness levels, each with rationale and citations
- Gap Prioritization Matrix
- Alternative prioritization or research direction
- Candidate Research Questions
- Experiment Framing Block
