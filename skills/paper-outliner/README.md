# Paper Outliner Skill

A high-rigor reasoning module for producing structured, standards-driven outlines for research papers and manuscripts. This skill explicitly aligns claims to evidence, surfaces missing evidence, suggests figure/table strategies, flags weak sections, and provides a publication readiness checkpoint. Outputs are designed for direct use by Reviewer Mode and for seamless handoff from Statistical Analysis.

- **Purpose:** Deliver explicit, evidence-linked section breakdowns, outline templates, and Reviewer Exposure Block for manuscript preparation, collaborative writing, and journal submission.
- **When to Use:** After statistical analysis, during manuscript preparation or collaborative writing.
- **When Not to Use:** If study results or research topic are missing, ambiguous, or unreviewed.
- **Related Standards/Assets:** `shared/paper-structures.md`, `skills/statistical-analysis/skill.md`, `skills/reviewer-mode/skill.md`, `standards/output-contracts.md`
- **Upstream:** Statistical Analysis (Paper Claims Block)
- **Downstream:** Reviewer Mode
- **Handoff Artifact:** Reviewer Exposure Block (machine-readable: weak sections, missing evidence, likely reviewer concerns)
- **Reasoning Features:** Claims-to-evidence linkage, missing evidence surfacing, figure/table strategy, weak section flagging, publication readiness checkpoint, structured handoff for downstream skills
