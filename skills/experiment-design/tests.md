# Experiment Design Skill — Behavioral Validation Tests

- Does the skill define all variables and controls explicitly and justify their selection?
- Does the skill generate both a primary and alternative design, with rationale for each?
- Is the tradeoff matrix explicit, actionable, and relevant to the designs?
- Are confounders, constraints, and validity threats surfaced and discussed?
- Is the risk register present and realistic?
- Are explicit assumptions and unresolved uncertainties reported?
- Is the Protocol + Schema Handoff Block structured and machine-readable?
- Is the experiment plan reproducible, auditable, and compliant with `standards/output-contracts.md`?
- Is the output suitable for direct use by Protocol Writer and Dataset Schema Designer skills?
- Does the skill request clarification if the research question or constraints are ambiguous?
- Does it handle errors and missing data gracefully, reporting them explicitly?
- Does the skill avoid generic, incomplete, or unjustified designs?
