# Experiment Design Skill

A high-rigor reasoning module for generating, evaluating, and documenting robust experimental designs based on prioritized research gaps. Produces both primary and alternative designs, surfaces confounders and validity threats, and generates structured handoff artifacts for protocol and dataset planning.

- **Purpose:** Generate and justify primary and alternative study designs, surface confounders, constraints, and align with analysis plans.
- **When to Use:** After gap identification with an Experiment Framing Block, before protocol writing or dataset schema design.
- **When Not to Use:** If research question is unclear, constraints are undefined, or upstream handoff is missing.
- **Related Standards/Assets:** `skills/gap-finder/skill.md`, `standards/skill-spec-template.md`, `shared/experiment-templates.md`
- **Upstream:** Gap Finder (Experiment Framing Block)
- **Downstream:** Protocol Writer, Dataset Schema Designer
- **Handoff Artifact:** Protocol + Schema Handoff Block (structured for downstream skills)
- **Reasoning Features:** Design alternatives, tradeoff analysis, risk register, analysis-to-design alignment, explicit assumptions and uncertainties
